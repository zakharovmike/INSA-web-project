Welcome page
	Where am I, what can I do here, why should I do it?

	Goal: 
		Let the visitor know their are on an exchange program information website.
		That they can find offers and information about the procedure.
		Because they want to find information (or are required to).

	Content:
		School logo and name
		* hover logo to reveal location details and copyrights and shit
		Background image
		"Departs a l'etranger"
		"Centre d'information pour votre semestre a l'etranger"
		* Notification(s) reminding of important due dates (general or uni specific). Though this data will be on the procedure page too.
		Our offers
		Procedure
		Why do it
		Contact

Our offers
	Goal:
		Provide an intuitive way to find corresponding university offers.
		Provide relevant information about a given university

		corresponding = "I'm in 3MIC, show me things that only apply to MIC people"
		relevant = only information for exchange students, and only classes that are similar to INSA

	Content:
		find university page:
			selector for year
			selector for spec
			map showing countries with offers, when clicked, zoom into country to display universities

		university page:
			title of uni
			pictures
			some relevant data (number of students, location - urban or nah, available places, a contact person, ...)
			list of courses appropriate to given year and spec (click on course to see details - accordion style)
			notes from students who have previously done their exchange there

Procedure
	Goal:
		Provide a guide for how to successfully go about setting up an exchange semester
		Provide access to all the necessary documents

	Content:
		step-by-step recipe for the things that need to be done (with dates inline)
		important date list
		document stash
		* checklist allowing you keep track of where you are (local storage)

Why do it
	Goal: Pursuade the user that doing an exchange semester is something to look forward to.

	Content:
		reasons why doing a semester abroad is benefitial to their education/something they might be interested in
		testimonials from students that really benefited from it

Contact
	Goal: Provide contact details for all the people the user might need, both setting up the exchange and during.

	Content:
		* selector for getting the right contact
		contact info (name, email, phone, reasons for which this person should be contacted) - click on card to contact directly (name, email, subject, message)
